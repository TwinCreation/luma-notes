Our Privacy Philosophy

Luma Notes is built on a foundation of absolute privacy. We believe that your digital notes are your private thoughts, and they should never be accessible to anyone but you.

Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect, receive, or store any personal data. Our app operates in a "Silent Environment":

  • No account registration is required.
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, IDFA) are logged.
  • Your notes and checklists are encrypted by your OS and stored locally.

Your Rights

Since we do not hold any data on our servers, you exercise your rights (Access, Deletion, Portability) by managing your notes directly within the app UI. You are the master of your information.
