Overview

Luma Notes is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your notes and personal content remain entirely on your device. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your notes, messages, or files.

However, we may collect limited technical data automatically through third-party services:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and configuration
  • App usage events (feature interactions)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This data is non-personal and used only to improve app performance and stability.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use the following services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand general app usage
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to detect and fix crashes

These services may process anonymized data such as device information, usage behavior, and crash reports. We do not use this data to personally identify users.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand feature usage

Data Sharing

  • No personal data is sold or shared
  • Limited technical data is processed by Firebase services
  • No advertising or marketing tracking

Data Retention

Analytics and crash data are retained only as long as necessary for debugging and improvement purposes, according to Firebase policies.

Security

We use standard Android security practices and rely on secure infrastructure provided by trusted services.

Your Rights

  • You can use the app without providing personal data
  • You can uninstall the app at any time
  • You control all locally stored data

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected within the app.
